My name is Robert Lu and I'm currently a third year undergrad student studying computer science at UCSD. I like to code simplistic yet robust software.
I first realized my interest in programming when I joined my high school robotics team. I remember being baffled of all the possible movements and actions the robot could perform with just some simple lines of code. Since then, I taught myself how to program and worked my way up, eventually becoming promoted to head developer of the team. By that time, I had resolved to pursue a career in computer science. I have worked with many different platforms and languages. Two of my favorites are Android and web development because of the accessibility they provide both the developer and the users. I enjoy front end development for the impact it makes on people's impression of your product. At the same time, I also respect back end development for holding everything together and the amount of problem solving that goes into both in order to make the whole product work.
